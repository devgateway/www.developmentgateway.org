Open Schools Kenya

An initiative to engage with the community of Kibera to collect and combine citizen- and government-sourced data, convert this information into an interactive and accessible format, and provide a feedback mechanism for parents and teachers to improve education opportunities. Open Schools Kenya is a joint project of GroundTruth Initiative, Map Kibera, DG, Feedback Labs, and the Gates Foundation.
