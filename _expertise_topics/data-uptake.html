Understanding Government Data Use in Nepal

Development Gateway, in partnership with DFID Nepal, conducted a detailed study of government data use use. Drawing on the perspectives of 60+ government officials, donor staff, CSO operatives, and academics, to paint a picture of the government data “ecosystem.” The aim was provide useful insight on data uptake for both Government of Nepal and the development data community at large.

International Aid Transparency Initiative

A member of the Steering Committee and TAG since 2011, DG is working to promote wider adoption of the IATI Standard, to improve the quality of data published, and is iterating on a tool to integrate the registry alongside existing organizational data.
