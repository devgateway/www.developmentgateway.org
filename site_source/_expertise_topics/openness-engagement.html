Ensuring that people can access and use development data for good.

Data is important, but only matters for development if people can use it effectively.

DG works with partners to make data open, accessible, and useful. We build open data tools, specialize in international data standards (like IATI, OCDS, and EITI), and offer trainings that help people in governments and organizations use data and tech to improve their work.

{% endmulticols %}

Case Study: Open Schools Kenya

An initiative to engage with the community of Kibera to collect and combine citizen- and government-sourced data, convert this information into an interactive and accessible format, and provide a feedback mechanism for parents and teachers to improve education opportunities. Open Schools Kenya is a joint project of GroundTruth Initiative, Map Kibera, DG, Feedback Labs, and the Gates Foundation.

Open Schools Kenya

DG and GroundTruth Initiative engaged with the community of Kibera to collect and combine citizen- and government-sourced education data, make this information interactive and accessible, and provide a feedback mechanism for parents and teachers. This work was completed via Feedback Labs as a Gates Grand Challenge Winner. Visit Open Schools Kenya.

AMP Good Practices Workshop

An annual gathering of DG partner government officials that use the Aid Management Platform. These workshops include technical knowledge sharing, hands-on data sessions, and case studies that help our partners use aid information to better inform policy, improve planning, and promote aid coordination.
